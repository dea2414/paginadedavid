ISU General Computer Science Requirements

The B.S. in Computer Science (General Sequence) requires a minimum of 83 credit hours:

  • Core Courses (24 cr): COM 223, ENG 249, IT 168, 179, 180, 191, 214, 225, 261, 279
  • Advanced IT (30 cr): IT 326, 327, 328, 378, 383, 386 and 6 cr from IT 398
  • Mathematics (9 cr): MAT 145, 146, 260
  • Electives (9 cr): Choose 9 cr from IT 244, 276, 329, 330, 340, 343, 348, 351, 352, 353, 355,  356, 367, 382, 385, 388
  • Lab Science (8 cr): Any 2 courses from BSC 196, 197; CHE 140, 141; PHY 110, 111

For full details, see the official ISU catalog page .
